About Us

Pick-A-Book bookstore was founded by Shivi Rawat,Astha Bhatt and Ramandeep Kaur on the 1st June 2021. The aim of Pick-A-Book bookstore is to make more varieties of books available to the locals and also in the same time to incalcate good reading habits among the population. Pick-A-Book currently offers a vast genre of books ranging from Thriller, Children, Fiction, Nonfiction and Cookbooks, just to name few. The books are available online to satisfy the free reading and book needs for all of our customer. So, what are still waiting for? Do browse through our exciting catalogues of books or drop by our bookstore yourself to pick up an exciting book of your choice today.
